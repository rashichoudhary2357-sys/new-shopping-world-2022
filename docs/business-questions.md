# Business Questions Answered — New Shopping World Report 2022

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data for 2022 using Excel dashboards.  
Below are the key business questions explored and answered through the visuals.

---

## 1. Compare the sales and orders using a single chart  
- Monthly Orders vs Sales trend to understand revenue pattern and order volume.

## 2. Which month had the highest sales and orders?  
- Identify peak performance month of the year.

## 3. Who purchased more in 2022 — Men or Women?  
- Gender-wise comparison of orders and sales.

## 4. What are the different order status categories in 2022?  
- Delivered, Returned, Refunded, Cancelled — and their distribution.

## 5. List top 10 states contributing to total sales  
- State-wise analysis to find the highest revenue contributors.

## 6. What is the relation between age and gender based on number of orders?  
- Age groups (Teenager, Adult, Senior) vs Gender comparison.

## 7. Which sales channel contributed the maximum sales?  
- Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Ajio — analyzing their share.

## 8. What is the highest selling category?  
- Category-wise performance to identify top product segment.

---

These questions were answered using PivotTables, slicers, charts, and interactive Excel dashboard elements.
